About Us

Wendy’s Garden is a small garden shop that offers a variety of plants, flowers, and gardening supplies. Our goal is to help people create a beautiful and relaxing green space in their homes. We are passionate about nature and committed to providing healthy plants and friendly service to our customers. At Wendy’s Garden, we believe that plants bring happiness, freshness, and life to every place.
